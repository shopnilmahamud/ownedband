About Owned

Owned is a Bangladeshi rock band that was formed by drummer A.K. Samee and bassist/vocalist A.K. Ratul in Dhaka in 2007. Over the years, the band performed in a lot of local gigs and released 5 singles which created a loyal following. Despite the line up changes, the band is still going strong and are finally releasing their full length debut album titled - 'One'.
